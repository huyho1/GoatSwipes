ABOUT US

College can be hard sometimes, and GoatSwipes is here to swipe away your stress. GoatSwipes is more than just a meals exchange service: we connect WPI students through reselling excess meal swipes to other students in an efficient and secure way. Created by a group of 5 outstandings students at the WPI GoatHack 2024, GoatSwipes aims at easing food security for upperclassmen, and further promoting our community's wellness as a whole.
